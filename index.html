About Us

The Peruvian Student Association brings together Peruvian and non-Peruvian students at Hunter College and across CUNY. Through food festivals, cultural dance workshops, and more, we build community by sharing the beauty of Peruvian culture while serving as a connector and encouraging other campuses to create their own PSAs.

And yes. We all LOVE Peruvian food!
